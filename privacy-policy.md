# Privacy Policy for OmniSearch

**Last Updated:** January 9, 2025

---

## Overview

OmniSearch is designed with a strong emphasis on user privacy. The application does not require account registration and is intended to operate without collecting personal information on OmniSearch-managed servers.

---

## Information Collection and Use

OmniSearch does not request or intentionally collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

The application does not operate its own analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking systems, or profiling mechanisms. Any data stored by the app is maintained locally on the user’s device.

---

## Search Functionality

OmniSearch functions as a client-side interface that forwards user-entered search queries directly to the selected third-party search engine.

- Search queries are transmitted directly from the device to the chosen provider  
- OmniSearch does not store, log, intercept, or analyze search queries on external servers  

---

## Local Data Storage

For usability purposes, OmniSearch stores certain information locally on the user’s device. This may include:

- Search history  
- Favorite or pinned search engines  
- Application preferences (such as theme or default engines)  

This data remains on the device and can be removed at any time through application settings or by uninstalling the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

OmniSearch provides access to third-party search engines and websites. When users interact with these services, data is handled according to the respective privacy policies and terms of those third parties.

OmniSearch does not control and is not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

---

## Children’s Privacy

OmniSearch is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
